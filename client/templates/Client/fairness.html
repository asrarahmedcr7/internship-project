Fairness and Bias

Description:

 These metrics are based on the Demographic Parity concept which suggests that the tendency of the model to reject a candidate should be equal across demographic groupings like Gender, Location, Age etc

Interpretation:

 Ideally, Demographic Parity should be the same for men and women i.e. both genders should have an equal probability of getting rejected by the Authentication Algorithm.

Variability across between men and women would suggest that, all other factors remaining the same, the model is rejecting one gender more than than the other.

Quantifying Demographic Parity, will also help leaders' quantify the extent of "discrmination" built into the algorithm.

A significant divergence in the Demographic Parity score indicates the Gender or Location or Age Group with the highest DP value is being discriminated against by the Algorithm, hence the AI Service provider needs to >> 1. Re-evaluate the impact of these parameters on the model (ideally there should not be any). 2. Check for unbalanced data corresponding to these features in the training data
