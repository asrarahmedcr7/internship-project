Tracking Talent AI Effectiveness: Why now?

With the increasing adoption of AI across enterprise use-cases, end-users have an urgent need to meaningfully assess the effectiveness of the enterprise Machine Learning models.

Effectively measuring the accuracy and precision of AI products is critical to ensure the Reliability, Scalability, and Safety of Enterprise AI initiatives.

This assessment helps uncover model performance issues, optimize resource use, and ensure compliance with regulatory standards, all while minimizing operational risks.
